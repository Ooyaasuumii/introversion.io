INTROVERSION.IO is a techno, house, and general purpose rave blog. Though the primary focus will be to share the tracks that are currently burning holes through my speakers and shaking my neighbor's walls, I won't hesitate to post about an amazing mix, label, or even show (NYC based). Posts will be added once or twice a week, but who knows, it's possible I find a new artist and decide to flood the site with their entire discography. As much as possible, I would to link to the artist's soundcloud, insta, twitter, youtube, myspace, whatever... so if I've missed a good link in a blog post, please shoot me an email or DM on Instagram.


When I first sat down to code this website, I was, as could easily be guessed, listening to techno. While I laid the HTML, CSS, and JS groundwork, one artist repeatedly came up in my shuffle: Introversion. Out of laziness and the need to test site functionality, I decided to place this name in the header and worry about changing it later. As the website came together over the following days, the minimal, black and white theme seemed to fit the temporary name. It's likely that I will change this later, but for now, it remains.

Lastly, I want to make it abundantly clear that this is an extremely informal blog and in no way a review.
